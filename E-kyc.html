e-KYC

Tache Technologies provides an electronic process of enabling an entity to
‘know your customer’

Features of our e-KYC

CIP Verification:

Customer Identification Procedure (CIP) at Tache Technologies identifies and verifies the true identity of a person.

Address Verification:

At Tache Technologies, customers’ address is verified by the team before making it available to the client.

Consent-based Investigation:

Tache Technologies carries out e-KYC authentication of an individual by obtaining the customer’s Aadhaar number with his/her consent.

Regulator Friendly:

Tache Technologies provides transparency and flexibility to its clients by facilitating customisations as per clients’ preferences.

Risk Management:

Risk management features of Tache Technologies include determination of customer’s risk, protection from frauds and accuracy of customer details.

Eco-friendly Approach:

Tache Technologies allows a paperless and easy-breezy approach of managing large documents by following the complete process digitally.
