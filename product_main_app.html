Mobile Application Features(Read More)

  • Tache Technologies exceeds all expectations when it comes to simplifying the process of survey data collection and authentication.
  • Features such as hassle-free uploading of photos, videos and background audio recordings of survey locations in all formats improve mobile data collection of survey audits as well as provide better audit results.
  • For easier data authentication, Tache Technologies provides survey auditors the advantage of authenticating their survey data and reports by validation through their E-signature and proof of audit.
  • Tache Technologies offers technology-oriented solutions such that a particular site of audit can be tagged and the auditors can navigate to the exact location just by simply tapping the icon.
  • Hence, locating a specific auditing location is no longer a hectic task for the auditors.
  • Smartphone features like GPS and route maps are inculcated within the app so that the auditors easily navigate to survey locations.
  • With Tache, finding audit locations is no longer a time-consuming and challenging task for the auditors and the project schedulers can easily coordinate with the auditors on calls.
  • Flexibility is essential to any mobile app as a platform that is high on flexibility offers enjoyable UI experience and performance.
  • Tache Technologies provides customization facilities in survey audits by allocating relevant questionnaires to a particular group of users.
  • Such a questionnaire consists of various MCQs based on converging logics in order to obtain authentically factual outcomes.
  • To reduce ambiguity, only the required set of questionnaires is open to the sampling under consideration, which helps in acquiring well-precise information from the users.
  • In this technological era where information technology is ubiquitous, the importance of cyber security is on the rise.
  • Tache Technologies facilitates its users by providing reliable and robust security services.
  • To provide a secure platform, a password-protected login is made mandatory for each user to access the features of the app and complete the survey.
  • Also, the users can log in to the system via their mobile number with the OTP facility in case a user forgets the password.
  • Along with secured authentication, it is essential that only authorised users can access the application.
  • Tache Technologies ensures that proper registration of each user is made feasible following a secured and elementary authentication procedure of the user interface.
  • Users can easily access the questionnaire under the guidance of the field auditors and they can create their own teams to add members.
  • Every individual is granted authority as per the team profile retained by the company.
  • The user interface of Tache Technologies is designed such that it facilitates survey audits in multiple languages as per client preferences.
  • The multilingual feature of Tache eradicates the instances that come to light where users find it difficult to interact with the platform due to language barriers.
  • With this incredible feature, Tache Technologies fulfils the requirements of users in different geographic locations as the application complies with different languages.
  • Users can easily read the content in their native language and utilize it by using this multilingual functionality.
  • In today’s digital world, networking is imperative to the growth of an individual or an organization.
  • To aid in the professional growth of the auditors, Tache Technologies allows its users to add members to the platform and create a team to collect data by tracking their activities.
  • To ensure that new users join the platform, an incentive is provided in the form of bonus points every time a user adds a new member to his team.
  • The process continues and the company rewards a suitable amount of bonus to every individual as the network increases.
  • Tache Technologies has eliminated the need for an active internet connection for working of the application and performing survey audits in remote areas or regions with low or no network.
  • It is unlike most other applications, that require a suitable working internet connection for functioning and any issues with the internet connectivity would hinder the user from using the app.
  • The app facilitates offline auditing and recording of responses as well as automatic uploading of captured responses on the availability of mobile network.
